  <p>We do not collect any personal information through the App. Specifically:</p>

  <p>The App saves your game progress locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to MidBoss or any third party. Uninstalling the App will delete this data.</p>

  <h2>Data Security</h2>
  <p>Because we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no user data stored on our servers to protect. All game save data remains on your local device and is subject to your device's own security settings.</p>
