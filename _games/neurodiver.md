Take on the role of ES88, a gifted esper employed by MINERVA, and hunt down the Golden Butterfly — a powerful rogue telepath who has hidden themselves away in the memories of Neo-San Francisco's citizens.

Armed with the Neurodiver, a genetically engineered psionic creature, ES88 dives into the minds of the city's residents and takes on the roles of familiar faces from the series: Jess Meas, gene-spliced hybrid lawyer; TOMCAT, notorious hacker; and Lexi Rivers, former police officer turned private eye.

Collect clues, manipulate memories, and piece together the truth in this story-rich point-and-click adventure. Features anime-inspired pixel art, a retro FM synth soundtrack by Ken "coda" Snyder, full voice acting, and multiple endings.
